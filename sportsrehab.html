ABOUT US

Sports Rehab is a club, a non- profit organization aims to focus on the various important aspects and issues in the sports industry which are left untouched and try to create a bridge between the existing scenario and required necessary solutions to improve the current state of the sports sector in terms of opportunities, facilities, and infrastructure and so on.

Sports Rehab aims to focus sports globally through mentoring, networking, education, incubating, and funding with the help of its association with TOTAL SPORTS LEAGUE. Dedicated to the virtuous cycle of wealth creation and giving back to the community, Sports Rehab focus is on generating and nurturing our next generation of sports persons.

This is the only platform connecting all sport lovers and sport fanatics of India to stay fit, play, connect, network, recreate, write and share their experience of the numerous sport activities that will be organized for all those who have the passion for sports in any form. Be it a player, fitness freak, Sports Blogger, Sports Equipment Manufacturer & Retailer and someone who has its own Sports Business- Sports Rehab welcomes you all. Guys be ready for a sporty ride ahead!

MISSION AND VISION

Sports Rehab envisions to scout for potential medal talent, to help identify areas of support, to work with all stakeholders to aid deserving talent and support the talent that represents the country and wins medals. It strives to compliment the efforts of the Indian Government and various Sports Federations in identifying the best and most deserving medal prospects at the international arena.

HOW WE DO IT ?

Sports Rehab will bring together eminent sportspersons, business leaders, sportswriters and talent scouts to identify emerging athletes, to understand their training needs and requirements and then to support their talent by systematically and scientifically overseeing and creating a benchmark for the progress of athletes and provide the possible resources to them so that they will have access to the best coaching and training facilities and to exploit scientific aids including planned nutrition & diet.
